About Haribhari

At Haribhari, our roots run deep, nurturing a passion for the environment that has grown into a thriving commitment to preserving the planet for future generations. We are a dedicated non-governmental organization (NGO) with a singular mission: to make the world greener, one tree at a time.

Our Mission

Haribhari's mission is to restore and conserve the planet's precious green spaces. We believe that by nurturing the Earth's natural beauty, we can foster a healthier, more sustainable future. Our commitment to this mission is unwavering, and it drives us to take concrete steps toward a greener world.



Our Vision

At Haribhari, our vision is deeply rooted in the belief that a flourishing planet is not a luxury but a fundamental human right. We envision a world where nature and humanity coexist harmoniously, where the beauty of our environment is preserved for generations to come.
